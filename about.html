About DeerInChase

Hello!

I’m Kaz Harte, also known as DeerInChase online. I'm a digital artist who specializes in bringing fantasy characters and settings to life with both 2D and/or 3D mediums.

A lifetime of playing Dungeons & Dragons and consuming weird free games off Itch.io has inspired me to pursue a career in game design and game art, though I’ve also recently begun selling my artwork as keychains, stickers and prints at local craft markets and artist alleys.

I see games as a very unique and exciting medium we can use to share our human experiences and tell immersive stories. My favorite part of making them is seeing a world be brought to life through the collective effort of myself and, oftentimes, the group of classmates or friends I’m working alongside.

Currently, I’m studying art and game design at the University of California Santa Cruz and am on track to graduate in the fall of 2026.
